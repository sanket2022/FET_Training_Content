Celery Root

Celery root is a little intimidating to look at but it’s actually quite simple to prepare. You need a sharp knife to peel it because the bumpy surface makes using a peeler very hard. Slice the top and bottom so that you can stand it on a cutting board. Using your knife, make sure you take off all the slightly hairy brown peel. The inside of the root is cream colored so don’t stop peeling until all the brown peel is gone.
